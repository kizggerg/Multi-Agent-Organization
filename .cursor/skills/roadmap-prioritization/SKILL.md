# Roadmap Prioritization

## Instructions

1. Score each initiative on impact, urgency, effort, risk, and strategic fit.
2. Surface dependencies and sequencing constraints.
3. Highlight items with highest value per cost.
4. Recommend what to start, delay, or stop.
5. Flag decisions requiring VP sign-off.

## Output Template

- Prioritized list with rationale
- Cost vs value summary
- Dependencies and sequencing notes
- Deferred items and why
- Escalations and approval needs
